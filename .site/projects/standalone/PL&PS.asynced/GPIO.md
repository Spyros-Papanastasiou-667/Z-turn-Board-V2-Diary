## Pin numbers
As per [PL & PS: Standalone](README.md), pins are:

* CAN: MIO14, MIO15
* [User] LEDs: MIO0, MIO9
* Button: MIO50

Summary:

* Output: MIO{0,9,14,15}
* Input: MIO{50}
